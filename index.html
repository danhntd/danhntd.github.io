Thanh-Danh Nguyen

Computer Vision - Deep Learning - Data Science

Danh Nguyen obtained his B.Sc. (Honors) in Computer Science from University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNUHCM) in 2021. He currently works as a researcher at Multimedia Communication Lab., UIT, VNUHCM. Prior to that, he contributed to Sugimoto Lab., City Science Lab., and VietAI Organization as a research intern, data science engineer, and teaching assistant, respectively. His research interests are Computer Vision and Applications, Deep Learning and Data Science.
