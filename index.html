Thanh-Danh Nguyen

Danh Nguyen is currently a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Information Technology (UIT), Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City (VNUHCM), where he also received his B.Sc. (Honors) in 2021 and M.Sc. in 2024. He is a researcher at the Laboratory of Multimedia Communications, UIT, VNUHCM. Previously, he worked as a research intern at Sugimoto Lab, a data science engineer at City Science Lab, and a teaching assistant at VietAI Organization. His research interests include computer vision, and deep learning, with a particular focus on image segmentation, low-shot learning, and generative models.
